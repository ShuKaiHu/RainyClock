Rainy Clock

Privacy Policy · Last updated June 2026

Overview

Rainy Clock is an iOS app that automatically adjusts your alarm time when rain is forecast along your commute route. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data is used and how.

Data We Do Not Collect

Rainy Clock does not:

  • Collect, store, or transmit any personal information to our servers.
  • Have a server or backend of any kind.
  • Use analytics or advertising SDKs.
  • Track you across apps or websites.

Data Stored On Your Device

The following settings are stored locally on your device using UserDefaults and never leave your device:

  • Home and work address text (used solely to look up your commute route)
  • Commute mode preference
  • Alarm time, rain lead time, and rain probability threshold

Third-Party Services

When you tap "Schedule Smart Alarm," the app contacts the following Apple services on your behalf:

  • Apple Maps (MapKit / CLGeocoder) – to convert your address text into geographic coordinates and calculate a commute route.
  • Apple Weather (WeatherKit) – to retrieve precipitation forecasts along your route.

Both services are provided by Apple Inc. and are subject to Apple's own Privacy Policy. Your addresses and location coordinates are sent directly to Apple's servers and are not shared with any other party.

Notifications

The app schedules local push notifications for your alarm. These notifications are processed entirely on your device. No notification content is sent to any server.

Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect any data from children under 13.

繁體中文

概覽

Rainy Clock 是一款 iOS App，會在通勤路線預報可能下雨時，協助你把鬧鐘提前。本政策說明 App 使用哪些資料以及用途。

我們不收集的資料

Rainy Clock 不會：

  • 將個人資料收集、儲存或傳送到我們的伺服器。
  • 使用任何後端伺服器。
  • 使用分析或廣告 SDK。
  • 跨 App 或網站追蹤你。

儲存在裝置上的資料

以下設定會使用 UserDefaults 儲存在你的裝置上：

  • 住家與公司地址文字，用於查詢通勤路線。
  • 通勤方式偏好。
  • 鬧鐘時間、雨天提前時間與降雨機率門檻。

Apple 服務

當你點選「排程智慧鬧鐘」時，App 會代表你使用下列 Apple 服務：

  • Apple Maps（MapKit / CLGeocoder）：將地址文字轉換為座標並計算通勤路線。
  • Apple Weather（WeatherKit）：取得通勤路線上的降雨預報。

這些服務由 Apple Inc. 提供，並受 Apple 的 隱私權政策約束。你的地址與座標會直接傳送給 Apple 的服務，不會提供給其他第三方。

通知

App 使用本機通知排程鬧鐘提醒。通知在你的裝置上處理，不會傳送到任何伺服器。

兒童隱私

本 App 不會刻意收集 13 歲以下兒童的資料。
