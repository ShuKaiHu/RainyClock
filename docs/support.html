Common Questions

Why did my alarm not ring like the built-in iPhone Clock app?

Rainy Clock uses iOS local notifications. iOS does not allow third-party apps to exactly replicate the full-screen system Clock alarm behavior. Make sure notifications are enabled and your device sound settings allow notification audio.

Why does the app need my home and work address?

The app uses your addresses to preview your commute route and check weather along that route. The app does not have a backend server and does not create an account for you.

Why is weather unavailable?

Route weather requires an internet connection and Apple Weather availability. If route or weather lookup fails, check your network connection and try again.

為什麼鬧鐘不像 iPhone 內建時鐘 App 一樣全螢幕響鈴？

Rainy Clock 使用 iOS 本機通知。iOS 不允許第三方 App 完全複製系統時鐘的全螢幕鬧鐘行為。請確認通知權限已開啟，且裝置音量與通知聲音設定正常。

為什麼 App 需要住家與公司地址？

App 會使用這些地址預覽通勤路線，並查詢路線上的天氣。App 沒有後端伺服器，也不需要建立帳號。

為什麼天氣資料無法載入？

路線天氣需要網路連線與 Apple Weather 服務可用。如果路線或天氣查詢失敗，請確認網路後再試一次。
